About

The Business Leaders of Tomorrow have been an established club at Riverside City College for more than 19 years. We have continually represented ourselves to our community and the college at wide, as a professional business club that provides students with an environment where members can learn leadership skills, responsibility, gain professional experience, discuss social and economic issues, and broader their horizons both on professional and personal levels. We are a club that provides its members these experiences through our business and

professional endeavors such as leadership retreats, business conferences, MBA forums, guest speakers, community outreach events, and workshops; just to name a few. We help support our members and the RCCD community at large with these events via grants from ASRCC and our personal fundraisers. We are an active and involved club on campus. Due to our continuous active involvement in campus and college events, we have received several Collegiate Point awards throughout our tenure.
